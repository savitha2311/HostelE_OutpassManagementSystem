About Us

At Hostel e-Outpass, we understand the challenges students face when it comes to obtaining permission to leave their hostel for various reasons. Whether it's a medical emergency, a family visit, or simply a break from the routine, we believe that the process should be streamlined and efficient.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide students with a convenient and hassle-free solution for obtaining hostel permissions. We aim to simplify the entire process, saving you time and eliminating unnecessary stress.
